A Bucket List for Glasgow

What’s a Bucket List?

If you have never heard of the term “bucket list”, it is a list of all the goals you want to achieve, dreams you want to fulfill, and life experiences you wish to experience before you die whilst living in this great city!

Why a Bucket List?

Whether you're new to the city or been in Glasgow longer than the Duke of Wellington himself, there is always something new to learn about your city.

Having a bucket list of all the things there is to do might remind you of what’s available so that you can go out and make sure you've check them all out!

It’s also a list to identify what you might want to do, whether it’s big or small, purposeful or random. It’s just like planning all the highlights for YOUR day-to-day life. Get ready for new-found enthusiasm knowing what you have planned ahead in Glasgow!

Even though this bucket list is already filled with goals by default, you can still find many new things to do and add to your own list.

Create Your Bucket List

Click the 'Bucket List' icon at the top of the page, have a scroll through the available places to visit and tweak it to your liking. You can remove and add things to your bucket until it 100% looks like what you want to do.
